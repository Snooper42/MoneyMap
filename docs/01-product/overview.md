# Product Overview

MoneyMap is a private, local-first personal finance app for people who want to manage their money without bank login, cloud sync, telemetry, or backend storage.

The app runs as static files in the browser. Financial data is stored locally in browser storage and can be exported through backups.

---

## Product promise

MoneyMap helps users manually track and review their financial life from one browser-based workspace.

The product is best understood as a local command center for:

- Spending and income review
- Accounts and net worth
- Budgets
- Goals
- Debt
- Investments
- Credit score history
- Subscriptions
- Merchant cleanup rules
- CSV-based transaction import

---

## Ideal user

MoneyMap is built for users who:

- Prefer manual control over automated bank sync
- Want a private finance tracker that runs locally
- Are comfortable exporting their own backups
- Use CSV files or manual entry to maintain financial records
- Want a broad money dashboard instead of a single-purpose budget tracker

---

## Current product stage

The audited build is `v0.1.12`.

It is feature-rich for a pre-1.0 app, but it should still be treated as an active development release. The product direction is strong. The next priority is stabilization, especially around routing, documentation accuracy, and reducing legacy patch-layer complexity.
