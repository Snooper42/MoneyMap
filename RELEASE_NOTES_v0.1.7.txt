MoneyMap v0.1.8

Focus: Dashboard and Accounts polish before framework migration.

Changes:
- Dashboard command center tightened with a next-action strip, cleaner quick actions, and account category summary chips.
- Accounts taxonomy expanded to cash, checking, savings, investments, retirement, property, vehicles, collectibles, jewelry, credit cards, mortgage, auto loans, student loans, personal loans, other assets, and other debt.
- Accounts UI tightened with more stable row hover states, clearer side summary, category chips, and cleaner desktop spacing.
- Favicons replaced with light and dark aesthetic orange-M SVG variants.
- Build/cache token updated to v0.1.8.

Privacy:
- Static GitHub Pages compatible.
- No backend, no analytics, no external APIs, and no change to existing localStorage data shape.
